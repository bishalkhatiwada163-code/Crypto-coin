General Questions

What is MyToken?

MyToken is a next-generation cryptocurrency built on Ethereum blockchain. It's designed to provide fast, secure, and decentralized financial transactions with minimal fees and maximum transparency.

How is MyToken different from other cryptocurrencies?

MyToken offers superior speed (millisecond transactions), enterprise-grade security, and a community-driven governance model. Our focus on user experience and real-world utility sets us apart from competitors.

Is MyToken secure?

Yes. MyToken uses military-grade encryption and has been audited by leading security firms. Our smart contracts follow the best security practices and are regularly updated.

Investment & Buying

How do I buy MyToken tokens?

You can purchase MTK tokens directly from our platform using MetaMask or other Ethereum wallets. Simply visit our Buy page, connect your wallet, select the amount, and complete the purchase.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept MetaMask, Trust Wallet, credit/debit cards, and bank transfers. You can also purchase through major exchanges that list MTK.

What's the minimum investment?

There's no minimum investment requirement. You can buy as few or as many tokens as you want, depending on your budget and investment goals.

Is there a limit to how many tokens I can buy?

No hard limit exists. However, large purchases may require additional verification for compliance purposes. Contact our support team for institutional purchases.

Wallets & Storage

Which wallets support MyToken?

Any Ethereum-compatible wallet can store MTK tokens, including MetaMask, Trust Wallet, Ledger, Trezor, and hardware wallets. We recommend hardware wallets for large amounts.

How do I securely store my MTK tokens?

For maximum security, use a hardware wallet like Ledger or Trezor. For smaller amounts, MetaMask with a strong password is secure. Never share your seed phrase or private keys with anyone.

Technical Questions

What blockchain is MyToken built on?

MyToken is an ERC-20 token built on the Ethereum blockchain. We're exploring layer 2 solutions and other chains for scalability.

How fast are MTK transactions?

MTK transactions are confirmed within 1-2 minutes on the Ethereum network. During peak times, confirmation may take slightly longer due to network congestion.

What are transaction fees?

MTK transactions include Ethereum gas fees, which vary based on network demand. We provide real-time fee estimates before any transaction.

Support & Issues

What if I sent funds to the wrong address?

Blockchain transactions are irreversible. Always double-check addresses before sending funds. Contact our support team immediately if this happens, and we'll do our best to assist.

Where can I get support?

Visit our Contact page to reach our support team. You can also join our community on Telegram or Discord for peer support.

How do I report a scam or suspicious activity?

Report any suspicious activity to security@cryptoverse.io immediately. We take security seriously and investigate all reports thoroughly.
